Frequently Asked Questions

  • How is the land cared for?
    Each property is managed by following regenerative practices wherever possible while also allowing access — nurturing soil, wildlife, and native ecosystems for future generations.
  • How do I access the land?
    Once you join, you’ll get digital directions and access info for each property. Come and go on your schedule—no gatekeepers, no red tape.
  • Where will Kinscape be available?
    We’re launching in select regions across the U.S. and expanding based on member demand. You’ll always know which locations are open before joining.
  • Is Kinscape family-friendly?
    Absolutely. Whether it’s quiet walks, camping with the kids, or peaceful getaways, our lands are selected with families in mind.
  • How is Kinscape different from public parks?
    Kinscape gives you less crowded access to private land with no permits, reservations, or limited hours. It’s nature on your terms, where peace and privacy come first. We're also not supported by the government.
